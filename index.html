About us

We believe that great coffee brings people together. Founded with a passion for quality and community, our shop is more than just a place to grab a drink — it’s a welcoming space to connect, relax, and enjoy the little moments. Every cup we serve is made with carefully selected beans, expertly roasted, and brewed to perfection. From rich espresso to smooth pour-overs and creamy lattes, our drinks are crafted with care. We also offer a variety of fresh pastries and light bites to complement your coffee experience. Whether you're stopping by for your morning boost, meeting friends, or settling in with a good book, we’re here to make you feel at home — one cup at a time. Come in, stay a while, and make yourself part of our story.

Menu

  • Coffe Cup

    A coffee cup is a vessel, typically with a handle, designed for drinking coffee and other hot beverages. They come in various sizes and materials like ceramic, glass, or paper, each suited for different settings and types of coffee.

  • Iced Caramel Macchiato

    The Iced Caramel Macchiato is a chilled coffee beverage featuring rich espresso shots layered over cold milk and vanilla syrup, finished with a sweet drizzle of caramel. It's a refreshing and creamy treat with a delightful balance of coffee and sweet flavors.

  • Iced Matcha Latte

    A refreshing and vibrant iced beverage combining the earthy, slightly grassy notes of matcha green tea with the creamy coolness of milk, often lightly sweetened for a balanced and energizing treat.

  • Croissant

    A croissant is a buttery, flaky, crescent-shaped roll made from a laminated dough, similar to puff pastry but with yeast. It's a popular pastry often enjoyed for breakfast.

  • Chocolate Cupcake

    A delightful mini chocolate cake, typically frosted and perfect for a single serving of sweet indulgence.

  • Chocolate Roll

    A Chocolate Roll is a delightful confection featuring a light and airy chocolate sponge cake that's spread with a creamy filling, often whipped cream or chocolate ganache, and then rolled into a log. It's sometimes coated with more chocolate for an extra decadent touch. Some variations also include fruit jams or other flavored creams.

  • Strawberry Shake

    A Strawberry Shake is a sweet and refreshing blended beverage typically made with strawberries, milk (or a milk alternative), and often ice cream or yogurt for a creamy texture. It's a popular treat known for its vibrant pink color and fruity flavor.

  • Mango Juice

    Mango juice is a sweet and refreshing beverage made from the pulp of ripe mangoes. Often enjoyed chilled, it's a popular tropical drink known for its vibrant flavor and smooth texture.

  • Orange Juice

    A refreshing and tangy liquid extracted from oranges, enjoyed as a beverage and known for its vitamin C content.
